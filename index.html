About MeO meni
Who
I am
Ko
sam ja

My passion for fitness and sport shaped my early life. Through years of competitive training and real experience with real clients, I built a coaching system that actually works — built on results, not promises.

Strast prema fitnesu i sportu oblikovala je moj raniji život. Kroz godine takmičarskog treninga i stvarnog iskustva sa pravim klijentima, izgradio sam sistem treniranja koji zaista funkcioniše — zasnovan na rezultatima, ne obećanjima.

In 2019 I became a nationally certified personal trainer. Since then I've worked with over 150 clients — from absolute beginners to competitive athletes — helping each one build a healthier, stronger and more confident version of themselves.

2019. godine sam postao nacionalno sertifikovani lični trener. Od tada sam radio sa više od 150 klijenata — od apsolutnih početnika do takmičarskih sportista — pomažući svakom da izgradi zdraviju, jaču i samopouzdaniju verziju sebe.

My training system combines modern sports science with continuous measurement, personalised programming, and daily accountability. No generic plans. No empty motivation. Just real, measurable transformation.

Moj sistem treninga kombinuje modernu sportsku nauku sa kontinuiranim mjerenjem, personalizovanim programiranjem i svakodnevnom odgovornošću. Nema generalnih planova. Nema prazne motivacije. Samo prava, mjerljiva transformacija.

My ApproachMoj Pristup
4-STEP
SYSTEM
4-KORAČNI
SISTEM

Every transformation starts with understanding your body. My four-step system ensures that every programme is built on data, not guesswork — and that progress never stops.

Svaka transformacija počinje razumijevanjem vašeg tijela. Moj četverokoračni sistem osigurava da je svaki program zasnovan na podacima, ne nagađanju — i da napredak nikad ne staje.

01
ASSESSMENT & MEASUREMENT
PROCJENA I MJERENJE

During our first session we measure everything: body composition, body fat percentage, hormonal indicators, posture and baseline flexibility. This data prevents injury and ensures optimal programming from day one.

Tokom naše prve sesije mjerimo sve: tjelesni sastav, procenat tjelesnih masti, hormonske pokazatelje, držanje i početnu fleksibilnost. Ovi podaci sprečavaju povrede i osiguravaju optimalno programiranje od prvog dana.

02
YOUR PERSONAL PROGRAMME
VAŠI LIČNI PROGRAM

The assessment results combined with your personal goals allow me to create a fully custom training and nutrition plan. No two clients receive the same programme — your biology and ambitions define everything.

Rezultati procjene u kombinaciji sa vašim ličnim ciljevima omogućavaju mi da kreiram potpuno prilagođen plan treninga i ishrane. Nema dva klijenta koji dobijaju isti program — vaša biologija i ambicije definišu sve.

03
TRANSFORMATION IN ACTION
TRANSFORMACIJA U PRAKSI

Consistent training, daily nutrition tracking and personal support will transform your body. Beyond the physical, regular exercise and healthy nutrition have a profoundly positive effect on mental health and confidence.

Redovan trening, svakodnevno praćenje ishrane i lična podrška transformisaće vaše tijelo. Pored fizičkog, redovne vježbe i zdrava ishrana imaju izuzetno pozitivan uticaj na mentalno zdravlje i samopouzdanje.

04
MONTHLY PROGRESS REVIEW
MJESEČNI PREGLED NAPRETKA

Every 30 days we repeat all measurements from Step 1. We review progress together, celebrate wins and optimise the programme if needed. Then we start a new cycle — moving continuously forward toward your goals.

Svakih 30 dana ponavljamo sva mjerenja iz koraka 1. Zajedno pregledavamo napredak, slavimo uspjehe i optimizujemo program po potrebi. Zatim počinjemo novi ciklus — neprekidno napredujući prema vašim ciljevima.
